## 💫 About Me

I'm **Alvin**, a Computer Science student at **Binus University Malang** with a strong interest in software development, UI/UX design, and community-driven research. I work across frontend and backend development with hands-on experience in tools like Next.js, and maintain a growing focus on design systems and data-driven thinking. Outside of coding, I enjoy fieldwork and social research — exploring how communities interact with technology and their environment.
